History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global event attracting thousands of innovators, developers, and thought leaders each year. Over the years, it has become a launchpad for groundbreaking ideas and collaborative innovation.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the global tech community. We believe in the power of collaboration, diversity, and future-focused thinking to drive positive change through technology.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Fields

Dr. Ada Fields, a pioneer in AI ethics, spoke at TechCon 2021 about responsible machine learning. Her research has influenced tech policy worldwide.

Marcus Lin

Marcus Lin, co-founder of a leading cybersecurity firm, shared insights on digital safety and startup growth at TechCon 2022.

Sophia Reyes

Sophia Reyes, renowned data scientist and mentor, delivered a powerful keynote on AI for social good in TechCon 2023.
